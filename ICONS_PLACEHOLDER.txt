IMPORTANT: Replace icon files before deployment!

The icon-192.svg and icon-512.svg files are SVG placeholders.

For production:
1. Create proper PNG icons (192x192 and 512x512)
2. Name them icon-192.png and icon-512.png
3. Replace the SVG files

See CREATE_ICONS.md for detailed instructions.
